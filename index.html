How It Works

1. Input Your Problem

Upload an image of your math problem or type it directly into the text box. Our system supports both handwritten and printed equations.

Choose the appropriate math subject from the dropdown menu to help our solver provide the most accurate solution method.

3. Get Step-by-Step Solution

Our solver will process your problem and provide a detailed solution with explanations for each step, helping you understand the methodology.

4. Learn and Practice

Use the solutions to learn new concepts and practice similar problems to improve your math skills.
